# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Welcome to the Textbook

This textbook explores the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics, focusing on how AI algorithms can enable physical interaction with the real world through humanoid robotic systems.

## What You'll Learn

In this textbook, you'll explore:

- The fundamentals of physical AI and how it differs from traditional AI
- The principles behind humanoid robotics and their applications
- How AI and robotics work together to create intelligent, embodied systems
- Practical applications of human-AI-robot collaboration
- The future of human-robot interaction and societal implications

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Advanced undergraduate and graduate students in robotics, AI, or computer science
- Researchers entering the field of physical AI
- Engineers working on robotic applications
- Anyone interested in the intersection of AI and physical systems

## How to Use This Textbook

The content is organized into modular chapters that can be studied independently, though we recommend following the sequence for optimal learning. Each chapter includes:

- Theoretical foundations
- Practical applications
- Exercises and problems
- Further reading and references

## Prerequisites

Readers should have:
- Basic understanding of machine learning and robotics concepts
- Familiarity with programming (preferably Python)
- Understanding of linear algebra and calculus
